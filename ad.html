Dive IN!

Lessons|Swim Teams|Lap Swim

Dive In is a program to improve swimming. It is supported by 4 pools: Crockpot, Secondary, Tertiary, and Wading.
You can take lessons there. If you pass Lv3, you can test for a lap swim. If you pass Lv5, you can test for a SwimTeam.
Here is a picture of a 2022 graduate student, Roleous Polyie Coberlyhog.

The DiveIn Website gives imformation on the program.
The DiveIn Social APP is where you can chat withe DiveIn officials.
Click on the "Play a game" tab to play digital water basketball.
